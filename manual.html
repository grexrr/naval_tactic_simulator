Introduction

Embark on a thrilling journey back in time with our 18th-19th century naval battle simulator. Experience the high seas like never before!

With a straightforward mechanism, all you need to do is navigate your ship. But don't be fooled by its simplicity. The key to mastering this game lies in understanding the dynamics of naval warfare.

Through this immersive game, we hope to give you a glimpse into the challenging life of an old-time captain, and help you appreciate the skills and expertise they needed to command their ships. Ready to set sail?

Operation

  1. Press 'w' or 's' to adjust speed.
  2. Press 'a' or 'd' to steer the ship.
  3. Press 'j' or 'l' to order your port (left) or starboard (right) crew to prepare for firing.
  4. Once the firing range is visible, maneuver your ship to aim at the enemy.
  5. Your brave crew you do all their best to engage the enemy ship.

Happy Hunting!
